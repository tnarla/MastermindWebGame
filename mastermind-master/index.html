What sequence of colors am I thinking of?

⇒ How to Play

I'm thinking of a sequence of four colors. Guess the sequence! Set up your guess and "Check it!" to see my response.

For each item in your sequence, I will show you either empty space, a white circle, or a black circle. A white circle means one of the colors you chose matches a color in my sequence, but its position in the sequence is wrong. A black circle means you got a color and its position right. A space means one of the colors you chose is not in my sequence.

I won't show you which of your colors is right or wrong. I will only show you blank spaces, white circles, and black circles in any order. With logic, you can break my code in 6 guesses or fewer! Four black circles means you win!
